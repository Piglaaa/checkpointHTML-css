About CR7

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is arguably one of soccer’s all-time greatest players. He is the game’s highest scorer on record and has been a super athlete from the beginning, when an 18-year-old Ronaldo became “the most expensive teenager in British football history” after Manchester United paid roughly $15 million for his transfer to the team. Currently a free agent, he was reportedly offered a $207 million-per-year deal by Saudi Arabian club Al Nassr FC.

Routine

Mornings

6 AM - 9H30 AM

After working out for about 2 hours, Ronaldo takes a nap for around an hour and a half. At 9:30 am, he fuels up with breakfast, which typically consists of high-protein foods, such as ham, cheese, and yogurt, as well as a glass of fruit juice. At about 10:30 am, he does another 2-hour workout session.

AfterNoon

3PM - 4h30 PM

At 3 pm, he turns in for yet another nap, wakes up at about 4:30, and has a second lunch, which again consists of nutrient-rich foods, like tuna fish with salad, eggs, and olives.

Evenings

7PM - 11PM

When he's finished his evening swim session, Ronaldo relaxes, and at approximately 11 pm, he turns in for the night so he can get several hours of undisturbed sleep.
